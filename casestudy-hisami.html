Bringing Hisami’s Vision to Life with a Seamless, Engaging Website & Brand identity

client

Hisami Shirahama | writer, author, creator

01

Logo Design

02

Web Design

03

WordPress + Elementor Development

Hisami is an author and publisher with a mission to share compelling stories with a global audience. To enhance her online presence, she needed a website that not only showcased her work beautifully but also provided an interactive and engaging experience for readers.

I designed and developed a website that blends storytelling with functionality, creating a visually appealing and user-friendly platform. Built with WordPress and Elementor, the site features a beautiful and serene section where users can browse her writings and immerse themselves in her creative, unique, and positive world. The design ensures easy navigation while maintaining a sophisticated and inviting aesthetic. This project was not just about building a website—it was about crafting an experience that resonates with Hisami’s readers and visually expressing her unique creative essence.

Creating a Warm & Engaging Digital Presence for a Global Audience

The goal of Hisami’s website was to craft a welcoming and informative platform that authentically represents her creative world. Designed to connect with a global audience, the site serves as a central hub where visitors can explore her work, stay informed about events and publications, and engage with her storytelling.

Key Objectives:

  • Build Connection: Offer an inviting space where readers can engage with Hisami’s work, leave comments, and follow her journey.
  • Simplify Navigation: Ensure a seamless and intuitive browsing experience, making it easy for users worldwide to explore her content.
  • Support Creative Growth: Provide an efficient, well-organized platform that allows Hisami to focus on her artistry while organically marketing her work.

Crafting a Meaningful Identity Through Logo & Branding Design

A strong brand identity is more than just visuals—it’s a reflection of a creator’s essence, values, and vision. For Hisami, I designed a logo that captures her inner beauty, creative mind, and the limitless possibilities of her work. The design embodies her storytelling spirit, breaking free from borders and limitations to inspire a global audience.

Beyond the logo, the branding design ensures a cohesive and memorable experience across all touchpoints. From color palettes to typography, every element is thoughtfully chosen to create a sense of warmth, creativity, and professionalism. The result is a brand identity that not only resonates with Hisami but also connects deeply with her audience.

What impressed me most was Seira's outstanding design sense and English language skills. Creating a logo and website was a completely new experience for me. During our meetings, she listened attentively and helped draw out my vision for the site. She clearly explained what I needed to do, and delivered everything by our target completion date, which was incredibly helpful.

As a writer, I also asked her to translate my written work (poems and stories) into English. Translation requires deep familiarity with both languages and an understanding of both cultures. Seira translated my work more beautifully than I could have imagined, achieving results that were far beyond my own English abilities and expression. Thanks to her English translations, my readership expanded from 'Japanese people' to 'people around the world.' This is truly remarkable.

Seira is always calm and full of wonderful ideas. Her natural sense and creativity are exceptional, and I felt completely comfortable entrusting my project to her. Of course, she also respected my opinions and feedback, so we could create together without compromising—I could always say 'I prefer this!' The finished website exceeded all my expectations and imagination, which makes me truly happy.

Even after the website was completed, she responded quickly whenever I wanted to make adjustments, and I'm deeply grateful for that. The site has already received feedback from readers, and my Instagram followers increased by about 80 people in just one week. I look forward to continuing to work with her for a long time to come!

- Hisami Shirahama | author, writer & creator
